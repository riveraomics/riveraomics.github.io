Advancing STEM Research and Education: Semester Recap for the RIVERA-OMICS group

May 27, 2024 - Authored by: Osvaldo D. Rivera-González
As we wrap up a successful academic semester at RIVERA-OMICS, we are thrilled to share the highlights and achievements from our concluding lab meeting. In an in-person session, led by Founding President and Principal Investigator, Dr. Osvaldo D. Rivera-González, the group marked significant milestones and set the stage for future advancements in academic research and educational endeavors.


Photo: The RIVERA-OMICS research group. (https://riveraomics.org/team)

RIVERA-OMICS is a molecular biology research group focused on omics science, therapeutics, and other related tangible biotechnologies with commercial potential [Learn more]. Importantly, OMICS is a branch of RIVERA Initiatives Inc. (riverainc.org), a Puerto Rico based 501(c)(3)-recognized Private Foundation focusing on STEM student education and professional enhancement. Through the resources allocated to the RIVERA-OMICS initiative, active undergraduates can develop and push research interests in singular and collaborative mentored programs either independently or in collaboration with their accredited resident institutions of higher education. Additionally, alumni & external contributors are welcomed to design research projects leveraging our resources and community of scientists.

Overall, the RIVERA-OMICS research group is designed to expand in proportion to the scientific curiosity and active engagement of its student members, alumni, and contributors, thereby fostering a dynamic and evolving scientific community and learning environment. PI Dr. Rivera is committed to identifying and developing local scientific talent, ensuring that the initiative advances scientific knowledge and contributes to the growth and professional development of our members and scientific trainees.

As we look forward to the next semester, we remain dedicated to research productivity and organizational transparency, seeking to nurture a collaborative and innovative academic and professional development environment. We invite all interested students, alumni, and external collaborators to read more below and to join us in our journey to excellence and scientific impact.

Key Milestones : 2024-01


As defined by raw research productivity.

  • Defined and advanced four core research pillars.
    Bioinformatics || Cancer Biology || Aging Biology || Clinical Genomics

  • Conducted initial wetlab experiments through student-driven collaborations.

  • Defined strategies for market research projects related to the core scientific pillars.

  • Began establishment of cancer therapeutics project that leverages external research collaborative agreements.

  • Initiated the writing of peer-reviewed papers covering critical topics such as mRNA expression and immune system regulation across cancers and aging progression.



Active Research Projects



Bioinformatics: Investigate landscape and interoperability of bioinformatics pipelines.
Cancer Biology: Investigate novel therapeutics and oncogenic mechanisms.
Aging Biology: Investigate aging mechanics and their commercial landscape.
Clinical Genomics: Investigate genomics and microbiome of Latin American population.

More info at https://riveraomics.org/projects

Looking Ahead: What's Next?


We have several exciting initiatives on the horizon including:
(1) continuation of open research opportunities in bioinformatics, market research, and scientific writing;
(2) remote lab meeting starting July 2024 with an initial journal club session;
(3) establishing and solidifying external research collaborative agreements for wetlab support;
(4) submission of significant grant proposals for research & education program development;
(5) development of Co-Op for non-traditional scientific roles such as Scientific Writers and Biotech Analysts.

Concluding Remarks


The RIVERA-OMICS academic group was thrilled to have had such a productive academic semester! Every venture was an opportunity to connect with local leaders and learn about new science! We are proud of our accomplishments and look forward to continuing our exciting projects and strengthening our collaborations. The RIVERA-OMICS team remains committed to advancing scientific research, fostering innovation, and supporting the growth of its student members. Stay tuned for more updates as we continue to grow and push on the boundaries of our scientific initiatives.

About RIVERA-OMICS:

OMICS (Omics Molecular Innovations and Commercialization Strategies), is an initiative built to develop omics-focused academic research projects and investigate the commercial landscape of novel biotechnologies and biomedicines. OMICS is a branch of RIVERA Initiatives Inc, a Puerto Rico 501(c)(3)-recognized Private Foundation that seeks to empower academia through comprehensive resources and collaborative research endeavors to advance educational excellence and innovation.
