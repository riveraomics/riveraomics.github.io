Conversation with Senior MSL @ Caris

07/11/23 09:14 - Authored by: Emmanuel Franceschini-Rosado
Being able to attend Dr. Negrón’s seminar was a very pleasant experience full of knowledge and experience in the different fields one can explore in science. From the pharmaceutical industry, to manufacturing, to working in a prestigious laboratory and exploring the world of a Medical Science Liaison (MSL) at Caris Life Science. Dr. Negrón was able to convey a thorough perspective of the different careers one can choose in the field of science.



Dr. Negrón conversing with students.


An MSL, is a subject matter expert who plays a key role in the pharmaceutical, biotech or medical companies whose role is to interact within the medical community, advise upcoming treatments, and promote current technology, which will help patients solve their medical needs. Dr. Negrón works for Caris Life Sciences (Caris) founded by David Dean Halbert, a next generation leading biotech company and precision medicine pioneer that helps develop and deliver outstanding solutions to revolutionize healthcare and improve human condition. Caris achieved the development of the world’s first and most comprehensive tumor profiling service, which provides a complete molecular blueprint and personalized guided treatment to cancer that improves the outcomes and saves lives.

In an ever-evolving world of scientific innovation and advancements in different fields, one can only do so much to contribute to science. There are a diverse pathways one can choose from to build a career in science such as: exploring the field of medicine, research, clinical technician, laboratory assistant, scientific writer, MSL, manufacture scientist, pharmaceutical MD or PhD, pharmaceutical technician, preparing yourself from a post baccalaureate to PhD and many other interesting careers to choose from. As a scientist you are expected to use the knowledge you have learned over the years, technology, and the opportunities to help contribute to the scientific world, give in when help is needed and search for innovative breakthroughs in the different areas that compose a scientific environment.

Overall, she was an exceptionally valuable resource brought to the institution by Dr. Rivera, and the event was an amazing opportunity to network with other science fellows.
